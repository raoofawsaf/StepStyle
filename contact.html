Standard shipping takes 3-7 working days depending on location. Express shipping items typically arrive within 48 hours inside tier-1 metropolitan cities.

Yes! We offer a hassle-free 7-day window for returns or size exchanges. Footwear must be unworn and inside original product packaging boxes.
